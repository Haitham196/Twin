# Fudan University — China Medical AI Training Program

## Institution
Fudan University — School of Medicine / AI in Healthcare Research Center

## Overview
Academic sessions at Fudan University covering research in medical AI, foundation models, and clinical applications.

## Key Topics
- Medical foundation models and their adaptation for clinical use
- AI-assisted diagnosis in pathology and radiology
- Regulatory framework for AI medical devices in China
- Federated learning for privacy-preserving medical AI

## Key Takeaway
Fudan's research demonstrates that large-scale medical foundation models, pre-trained on hundreds of millions of clinical records, can significantly outperform task-specific models when fine-tuned on limited local data.
