# United Imaging — China Medical AI Training Program

## Institution
United Imaging Healthcare (联影医疗) — Leading Chinese medical imaging equipment manufacturer

## Overview
Visit to United Imaging's research and manufacturing facilities, covering AI integration in medical imaging hardware and software.

## Key Topics
- AI-enhanced MRI, CT, and PET-CT imaging systems
- Automated image reconstruction reducing scan time while improving quality
- Intelligent workflow: from patient scheduling to report generation
- Cloud-connected imaging platform for multi-site health systems
- AI quality control for imaging parameters

## Key Takeaway
United Imaging demonstrates how AI can be embedded at the hardware level, not just as software add-ons, enabling imaging quality improvements that are impossible through software alone.
