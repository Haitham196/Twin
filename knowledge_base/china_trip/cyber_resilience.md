# Cyber Resilience — China Medical AI Training Program

## Overview
Session on cybersecurity and resilience for healthcare AI systems and hospital IT infrastructure.

## Key Topics
- Threat landscape specific to healthcare: ransomware, data theft, AI model poisoning
- National healthcare cybersecurity standards in China
- Zero-trust architecture for hospital networks
- AI system integrity: protecting models from adversarial attacks
- Incident response playbooks for clinical systems
- Business continuity for critical healthcare applications

## Key Takeaway
Healthcare AI systems introduce new attack surfaces beyond traditional hospital IT. Protecting AI models (against poisoning and evasion attacks) requires security measures distinct from protecting data and networks.
