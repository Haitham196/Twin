# Medical Foundation Models — China Medical AI Training Program

## Overview
Sessions dedicated to large-scale foundation models trained on medical data and their clinical applications.

## Key Topics
- Architecture of medical multimodal foundation models (text + imaging + genomics)
- Pre-training on Chinese national health record datasets
- Adaptation strategies: fine-tuning vs. prompting for clinical tasks
- Performance comparison with GPT-4 on medical benchmarks
- Deployment challenges: latency, cost, regulatory approval

## Key Takeaway
Chinese medical foundation models trained on population-scale data show strong performance on Chinese-specific disease patterns and clinical workflows, highlighting the importance of locally-trained models for regional healthcare systems.
